Current

I am currently working on developing natural language processing and related AI technologies to solve social issues. Before assuming my current position, I was a PhD student (then a postdoctoral researcher) at Tokyo Institute of Technology. I worked with Prof. Takenobu Tokunaga and Prof. Simone Teufel on AI technology to assist language learners to write good argumentative essay in English. Then, I joined SmartNews as an ML Engineer where I developed NLP and information retrieval technologies to understand news and users' interests.
