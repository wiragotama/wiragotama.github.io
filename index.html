Jan Wira Gotama PUTRA

Machine Learning Engineer, Natural Language Processing Group | ExaWizards

I am currently working at ExaWizards to solve social issues by using natural language processing and related AI technologies. Previously, I worked as an ML Engineer at SmartNews where I developed NLP and retrieval technologies to understand news and users' interests. I completed my PhD (+postdoc) at Tokyo Institute of Technology under Prof. Takenobu Tokunaga and Prof. Simone Teufel, where we worked on AI technology to enable language learners writing coherent argumentative essays in English.
