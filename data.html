ICNALE-AS2R ICNALE Annotated with Argumentative Structure and Sentence Reordering
This language resource is a collection of English learner essays which are excepted from ICNALE: International Corpus Network of Asian Learners based on two criteria: grammatical error free and moderately scored. Each essay is annotated with the argument structure and sentence rearrangement for improving essays.
